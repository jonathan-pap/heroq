    Inspect

    Click a cell to see what lives on it and the edges around it.

    Architecture

    Cells (col, row) hold per-quest data. Walls and doors are edges between cells, not cells. Floor is flat — rooms are defined by which cells share a roomId, and walls are derived from roomId changes plus any explicit walls array in the quest JSON.
